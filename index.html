About the brand

Built on trust. Powered by community.

RC Motogarage was founded to close the gap between high-end motorcycle gear and real Indonesian riders. From Arai and RS Taichi to local fit-outs and protective wear, every product is chosen to match terrain, weather, and riding style.

The brand grew from a single workshop into a household name across Greater Jakarta — known for same-day service, hands-on fitting, and campaigns that don’t just look good, but move people.
