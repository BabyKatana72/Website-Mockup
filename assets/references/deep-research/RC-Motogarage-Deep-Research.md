- **Core Categories:** Helmets, riding jackets, gloves, boots, balaclavas, waist bags, intercoms, armor/protectors, general motorcycle riding apparel and accessories
- **Brands Carried (verified by press/social):** AGV, Kushitani, HJC, Qwart Helmet, Tomica, Bilmola, XPD Boots, Cardo, Retouch, RS Taichi, Alpinestars/unverified
  - Sources: JPNN.com Nov 2020 (Kushitani),Instagram posts (AGV, HJC, XPD, Cardo, Retouch), TikTok (Tomica x Bilmola)
- **Target Customer:** Indonesian motorcycle riders — urban commuters, sport/touring riders, and enthusiasts seeking premium branded gear
- **Sales Channels:**
  - Offline stores in Jakarta, Bandung, Serpong, Surabaya
  - Online marketplaces: Tokopedia (multi-city official stores), Shopee, Blibli
  - Direct WhatsApp sales per city
  - Membership program with offline registration
  - Website: https://go.rcmotogarage.id/ and http://www.rcmotogarage.com/
  - Source: https://go.rcmotogarage.id/
